Why the name Creative Cog . Design?


As someone who has always been interested in psychology and design, I was thrilled to learn that there was a field that combines the two disciplines. Web development allows me to use both the analytical skills that I acquired while studying for my psychology degree and also my innate creative abilities. Hence, why I chose the name Creative Cog Design! Currently, I am looking for career opportunities that will allow me to bind my love for design and coding.


When I'm not working at my computer, you can find me by the water, reading about psychology and cognition, or hiking through the mountains or the deserts.
