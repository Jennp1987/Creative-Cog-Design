Uptalent - An Architectural Drafting Company

My Roles: UI/UX redesign and development.

Uptalent approached me with a problem to help them streamline and design a landing page for their newly chosen area of specialization. They wanted to improve the flow and content of the website while still making sure that it was staying aligned with their business proposition. They also wanted to create an experience that would easily guide the visitor to book a consultation with the owners.

My process was constructing user flows and mockups to optimize the UI of the website to create a better UX for potential clients. To further enhance the UX, I designed and developed a custom registration and login system.
